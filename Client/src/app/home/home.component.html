Welcome to SCCS

The Smoking Cessation Clinic system will help people that needs guidance and assistance in quitting smoking and provide a powerful means that enables them to take control of their health, improve their quality of life and improve the impact on public health. Also, it will provide smoking cessation clinics with a system that facilitates their work, increases their efficiency, and increases the spread of these clinics

Smoking Sessation Clinic JUST


The message:
Contributing to reducing smoking rates within the university by providing scientific awareness The harms of tobacco and smoking in all its forms, and to help those who wish to quit smoking by providing Medical advice

Objectives
  • Spreading awareness of the dangers of smoking in all its forms
  • Holding lectures and seminars by specialists on the importance of quitting Smoking from a health, psychological, financial and religious perspective
  • Participate in educating the community, especially school students, about the dangers of this scourge
  • Holding periodic activities to highlight the role of students in fighting this scourge

Vision:
Towards a smoke-free university community
